A Few Words About YUNGBLUD

Dominic Richard Harrison, known professionally as YUNGBLUD, is an English singer, musician and songwriter from Doncaster, South Yorkshire. He was born on 5 August 1997. He released his first EP, YUNGBLUD, in 2018, followed by the album 21st Century Liability. In 2019, he released the EP The Underrated Youth. His second album, Weird! (2020), reached number one on the UK Albums Chart.
