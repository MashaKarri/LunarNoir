Led Zeppelin: The Beginning

Led Zeppelin was one of the most influential and innovative rock bands of the 20th century. Formed in 1968, the band consisted of Robert Plant, Jimmy Page, John Paul Jones, and John Bonham. Their blend of blues, rock, and folk influences, combined with heavy guitar riffs and powerful vocals, revolutionized the music industry, paving the way for the hard rock and heavy metal genres. With legendary albums like "Led Zeppelin IV" and iconic songs such as "Stairway to Heaven," their music continues to inspire generations of musicians and fans.
